When you visit our website, we may automatically collect:

We use Facebook Pixel to track website interactions and optimize our advertising. This may collect:

For more information, see Facebook's Data Policy at facebook.com/privacy

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

5. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

5.1 What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit our website. They help us provide a better user experience.

5.2 Types of Cookies We Use

  • Essential Cookies: Required for the website to function properly
  • Analytics Cookies: Help us understand how visitors use our site
  • Marketing Cookies: Used for advertising and remarketing (Facebook Pixel)

5.3 Managing Cookies

You can control cookies through your browser settings:

  • Most browsers allow you to refuse cookies
  • You can delete existing cookies from your device
  • Note: Disabling cookies may affect website functionality

To opt out of Facebook tracking, visit: facebook.com/settings?tab=ads

We take the security of your personal information seriously and implement appropriate technical and organizational measures:
